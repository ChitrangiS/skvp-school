Academics at Sri Kalinga Vidya Pitha Higher Secondary School, DNK

We offer a broad and balanced curriculum designed to prepare students for higher education and future careers.

Curriculum Overview

  • Primary school education is offered following both NCERT and CHSE syllabus standards.
  • Science Stream: Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Mathematics, Computer Science
  • Arts(Upasastri) Stream: History, Geography, Political Science, Sociology,Darshan,Sahitya
  • Penguin Play School offers a joyful nursery experience with engaging games, fun learning activities, and a caring environment designed for early childhood growth and development.
  • Language Options: Odia,English,Hindi

Examination and Assessment

Continuous assessments, monthly exams, regular PTMs, unit tests, and annual board examinations are conducted to ensure consistent progress and deep understanding of every student.

Academic Support & Achievements

Remedial classes and counseling are available to help students excel academically. We are proud to have produced district toppers in Class 10 and Class 12, received awards from several higher authorities, and won prizes at state-level science exhibitions and competitions. These achievements reflect our strong academic support system and the dedication of our students and teachers.
