About Sri Kalinga Vidya Pitha Higher Secondary School

Founded in 2013, Sri Kalinga Vidya Pitha Higher Secondary School, DNK Umerkote (Nabrangpur district) is committed to nurturing talent and shaping future leaders through quality education and holistic development.
Offering classes from Nursery to Class 12 (Science) along with Upasastri, the school provides a strong academic foundation, modern teaching methods, and a disciplined learning environment.
With an excellent academic record over the years, it stands as one of the best schools in the entire region.

  • Quality education and strong academics
  • Experienced and dedicated faculty
  • Nursery to Class 12 (Science) + Upasastri
  • Hostel facility with hygienic mess
  • Modern classrooms and smart learning
  • Excellent academic results every year
  • Active sports culture and physical training
  • Focus on discipline and moral values
  • Safe and supportive campus
  • Holistic development through activities
