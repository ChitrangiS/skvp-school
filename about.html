About Shri Kalinga Higher Secondary School, DNK

Founded in 2013, Shri Kalinga Higher Secondary School, DNK Umerkote, in Nabrangpur district, has grown into one of the region’s most trusted institutions for quality education. The school is dedicated to nurturing young talent, shaping disciplined individuals, and preparing future leaders through a strong academic foundation supported by a holistic development approach. The institution offers education from Nursery to Class 12 (Science) along with Upasastri, ensuring a complete learning journey under one roof. With a well-structured curriculum, modern teaching methodologies, and a supportive environment, the school focuses on building confidence, character, and competence in every child. Students benefit from experienced faculty, value-based learning, smart classrooms, and a culture that encourages curiosity, creativity, and growth. With an excellent academic track record year after year, Shri Kalinga Higher Secondary School stands proudly as one of the best schools in the entire region, known for its discipline, strong values, and commitment to all-round student success. The school continues to inspire learners, empower families, and contribute to the educational excellence of the community.

We focus on academic excellence, sports, cultural activities, and moral values to nurture well-rounded individuals ready to face future challenges.

Our Vision

Our vision is to provide a learning environment that inspires academic excellence, discipline, and strong moral values. We aim to nurture confident and responsible individuals through holistic education, modern teaching practices, and active participation in sports and cultural activities. With supportive and well-disciplined hostel life, we strive to develop independence, teamwork, and good habits in every student, preparing them to become capable, compassionate, and future-ready leaders.

Our Mission

  • Achieve academic excellence with district toppers in Class 10 and 12.
  • Provide quality teaching through experienced and dedicated faculty.
  • Promote holistic development—intellectual, physical, emotional, and spiritual.
  • Encourage sports, athletics, and physical training for overall fitness.
  • Integrate yoga, meditation, and spirituality into daily learning.
  • Maintain a disciplined and supportive hostel environment.
  • Offer smart classrooms and modern facilities for advanced learning.
  • Develop strong moral values, character, and leadership qualities.
  • Create a safe, inclusive, and motivating school atmosphere.
  • Prepare future-ready students who contribute positively to society.
